Volunteer Work

If what I do at work is good for my community, then I am in the right place.
Cailenys Salazar

I discovered my love working with kids teaching at summer camps back in Venezuela at the age of 16. Children are loving, innocent, brave and always curious. Having an abundance of curiosity is a great quality and perfect to start to learning STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art and maths )!

On September 28, 2012, when Venezuelaâ€™s first satellite to successfully orbit and transmit to Earth had been sent into space, was one of the inspiring moments of my life! My engineering classmates and I collaborated with building satellite models to help students (elementary and secondary school) and teachers, understand the important moment for what our country was going through and to learn the science behind satellites and digital television. Often in libraries, we helped them discover these new technological capabilities through interactive presentations.

Arriving to Canada, I volunteered with ACC Business Excellence Summits featuring Disney Institute training. It's has been one of the most fun events where I have volunteered! We delivered gifts to the Ronald McDonald House in Edmonton, helping hospitalized children realize some precious moments.

Also, I became certified in Disney's Approach to Leadership Excellence, Employee Engagement, and Quality Service and earned my "Mickey ears"!
